About DC Gaymers

DC Gaymers is a vibrant LGBTQ+ gaming community based in Washington, D.C. We organize regular events like board game nights, RPG one-shots, tournaments, and socials that provide safe, inclusive spaces for queer gamers to meet, play, and connect. Open to all experience levels and identities, we believe gaming brings people together!
